Welcome to the Brain Decision Modelling Lab Whack-A-Mole Game!
Using the touch screen your task is to whack (touch on screen) as many moles as possible
You will score between 0 and 10 points, the more accurate the hit the more points
But sometimes the environment doesn't behave...
... and you will score more or less than you deserve
To continue press 'c', to quit press 'ctrl q'
If you need to pause while playing press 'p'
